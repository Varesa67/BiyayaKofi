FAQ

Common questions about our brews and space.

Do you roast your own beans?

Yes. We roast in small 5kg batches twice a week to ensure every cup is at peak freshness.

Do you offer non-dairy milk?

We offer Oatly Oat Milk and Almond milk for a small ₱35-₱50 add-on.

Can I book for private events?

Absolutely. We host birthdays, workshops, and corporate mixers. Contact us for rates.

Is there high-speed Wi-Fi?

Yes! We are remote-work friendly with fast fiber Wi-Fi and plenty of power outlets.

Where are your beans from?

Our core beans come from Atok, Benguet. We also feature seasonal roasts from Ethiopia and Brazil.

Do you sell whole beans?

Yes! We sell 250g and 500g bags in-store and can grind them for you upon request.
